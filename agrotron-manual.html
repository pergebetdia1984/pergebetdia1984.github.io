Let’s talk about the importance of these manuals

In order to be able to use our Deutz Fahr Parts Catalog in the most economical and correct way possible, we must know that it is very important to respect the service and maintenance period. The manufacturer’s recommendation to replace consumables with new ones should never be exceeded. In this way we will prevent parts wear, engine wear, we will have a good fuel consumption and engine power (kW / HP) will remain unchanged.

Example of the oil change intervals recommended by the manufacturer for Deutz Fahr Machines
– Engine oil 15.5 l (change every 500 h);
– Transmission oil 67 l (every 1,000 h);
– Hydraulic oil 60 l (every 1,000 h).

Through these timely services, we can prevent any problems that may occur in our machine.
